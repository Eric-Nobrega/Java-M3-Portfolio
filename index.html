City Secret Agent

Above is a short video of my game City Secret Agent. It is a game in which the user is placed in several different scenarios, such as a City Setting, Space Setting, Underwater, etc. The goal is to complete the game as fast as possbile, while eliminating all the enemies present on the map. There are several features that I am proud of, most namely the multiple saving, enemy patroling, custom pickup objects, etc.

Challenges & Overcoming Them

During the development of the game, I initially struggled with managing a project of this size, as there are several different classes, some inheriting from others. However, I feel as if now I am more than comfortable with this topic, aswell as other Object Oriented Programming as a whole. In summary, the development process of this game/project has helped to further my knowledge of Java, as well as other programming concepts.
